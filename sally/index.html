What is Sally?

Sally is a combat robot I built and designed mostly in two weeks (Two weeks, because of procrastination). One of my primary design goals was to build a robot that was particularly esoteric and unique. I'm fairly certain I have succeeded at this, as there is only one similar robot, MoZar, which mine shared a rough design with. As is visible in the picture, Sally is a dual outer vertical spinner, with the spinner driven by friction pulleys.
